Our startup sets out to develop an application that provides citizens, using simple user friendly tools, with a virtual opportunity for sharing their visual content over urban frontages through an augmented reality (AR) technique.

How It Works?


What one virtually writes, draws or paints to share is viewed in the real world through the lens of smart phone cameras and can be commented on and edited. While walking through city areas, people, using our application, can discover, over their phone cameras, a different urban experience reflecting others’ feelings and ways of thinking.


Using sensors such as accelerometer, GPS, gyroscope, solid state compass, images that have been generated and shared on the application, overlays people’s view of the real urban faces and facades and can be evaluated or even, without being removed, superimposed by new images.
